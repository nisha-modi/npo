Our Goal

Applications often rely on dependencies to provide functionality and improve application appearance, among other things. However, with hundreds of open source packages out there, many of which also rely on dependencies themselves, application maintence and security can get complicated.

Our goal is to optimize your application's dependency tree. We search your application for packages that could present vulnerabilities, and provide alternatives that will simplify future development and maintence.




How is Tree Score calculated?

We look at your application's dependency tree to find dependencies you are using which have multiple dependencies themselves. We use these packages descriptions to search for alternatives with similar functionality, but fewer dependencies.

The Tree Score for your package and its alternatives is calculated by analyzing the depth of the dependency branch. We favor packages that create shallow branches (ones whose dependencies don't rely on dependencies), and NPM's rating of the package, based on package Quality, Maintainability, and Popularity to calculate your packages, and our alternatives, Tree Scores.
